Test your trivia knowledge while playing a strategic 5-hole golf course. Each correct answer executes a shot, with your club and question difficulty determining distanceharder questions mean more yards!

How to Play

Select Your Club
Choose from Driver (280y), 3 Wood (230y), Iron (175y), or Wedge (90y) based on distance to the hole. Driver NEVER allowed on Par 3s! On Par 4/5s, Driver only available on Hard after your first shot.
Pick Question Difficulty
Hard gives 100% distance, Average gives 85%, Easy gives 70%. Higher risk = higher reward!
Answer Trivia
Get it right for full distance. Wrong answer = "SHANK" (only 25% distance). Be strategic!
Beware Overshoot
Hitting too far flies over the green!
Avoid Hazards!
Bunkers: -30% distance on next shot. Must use Iron or Wedge to escape!
Water: +1 penalty stroke and re-hit from previous position.
Putting
Within 30 yards = green! Auto-forced difficulty: 1st putt Hard, 2nd Average, 3rd+ Easy.
Power-Ups
50/50 (2x): Remove 2 wrong answers.
Hole Out (1x): When you have perfect distance into the flag (within 10y), answer 2 Hard questions to sink it!
Bomb (2x): Answer EasyAverageHard for 130% distance! (1 correct=5y, 2 correct=normal, 3 correct=BOMB!)
